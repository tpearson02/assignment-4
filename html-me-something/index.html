The Bucks Rebuild

2013-2014: The Rebuild begins..

The Milwaukee Bucks recruit Giannis Antetokounmpo with the 15th spot in the 1st round. They go on to have the worst record in the league Giannis' rookie year. This leads to new owners AND a new coach, Jason Kidd; and securing Jabrai Parker in the 2014 draft.

2015-2017: They did what??

After getting new management, having seasoned vets and acquiring new breakout stars, the Bucks moved up to the 6th slot in the Eastern Conference 2014-2015 season after 54 point blowout from Chicago Bulls. They go on to sign Greg Monroe and have an okay season. Following the 2016-2016 season, the Bucks pick up Malcolm Brogdon in the draft and trade Michael Carter-williams for Tony Snell. All for Jabari Parker to tear his ACL again in February 2017. This leads to them falling to the Raptors in six games and John Hammond leaving and Jon Horst promoting. To top it off, the Bucks get Eric Bledsoe for Greg Monroe, their 1st round pick AND 2nd round pick for the upcoming draft.

Still Buidling

2018-2019: Almost there

Jason Kidd gets FIRED. The Bucks go on the be the 7th in the East and lose to the Celtics in the first round of play offs. Mike Budenholzer is hired as new coach for four years in May 2018. Bucks secure Donte DiVincenzo at 2018 NBA Draft; with Brook Lopez signing as a free agent a month later.They go on to get Nikola Mirotic back in February 2019 but not after giving uo Thon Maker, Jason Smith and FOUR second-round draft picks. (That's until 2023) They go on to be "Number 1" in the East while defeating Boston and advancing to Conference Finals! First time since 2001.

Oh and Giannis wins MVP for the season...no big deal.
